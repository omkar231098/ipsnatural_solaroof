  • Grid Connected Solar System

    • Suitable for any customer with shadow free roof / ground area - Residential, Commercial and Industrial (C&I), Institutes (Schools, Colleges etc)

    • Exports excess electricity to grid, as per metering regulations

    • Product range from 1 kW to large MW scale

    • High efficiency mono crystalline solar panels

    • Best quality components - such as AJB, Module Mounting Structure etc - for maximizing output & system life

    • Remote monitoring on mobile and web interface

    • Tata Power full system warranty support for 5 years, extended warranty options available

  • Battery Based Solar System

    • Best suited for areas with grid outage/reliability problems, for homes and small commercial, industrial & institutional set-ups

    • Off-grid: Power critical lighting loads, without grid export functionality

    • Hybrid: Power heavy load, export excess generation to grid & can also complement DG sets

    • Product range upto 10kW

    • High efficiency mono crystalline solar panels

    • Multicolour LCD display on inverter to monitor system performance; and intelligent hardware enabling different power saving levels

    • Tata Power full system warranty support for 5 years

  • Micro inverter based grid connected system

    • Uses advanced micro-inverter technology instead of string inverters used regularly in grid connected solar rooftop

    • Advanced solar technology with compact design, higher generation & increased savings

    • Suitable for roofs having orientation challenge as well shading due to trees/building etc.

    • 1 microinverter per solar panel - Allows module level monitoring with no single point of failure

    • Electrical safety - No aggregation of high voltage Direct Current on the roof

    • Tata Power full system warranty support for 5 years, higher inverter warranty of 10+ years, extended warranty options available

Solar solutions installed on your roof or ground are ideal for harnessing the sun's energy. Not only do they save electricity costs, but also help you fulfill your role as a responsible citizen by contributing positively to the environment.

Over 25 years lifetime of solar, upto Rs 2 Cr+ savings for MSMEs and upto Rs 10 lakh+ savings for residential customers
Typically, payback period of 4-5 years
Grid independence against rising tariff: Electricity tariff has increased by more than 50% in 5 years' time in many states

Similarly, for Residential, upto 150+ tonnes of carbon emission mitigated over a lifetime of solar solution use, equivalent to the planting of upto 250+ trees

Solar rooftop system has no moving parts
